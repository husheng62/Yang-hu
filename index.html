Welcome to Computational Materials Research

At Empa's Computational Materials Research Group, we leverage advanced computational methods to design and discover novel materials with tailored properties for energy, electronics, and sustainability applications.

Our research combines first-principles calculations, machine learning, and multiscale modeling to accelerate materials discovery and understand materials behavior at the atomic level.

We collaborate closely with experimental groups to validate predictions and develop new computational tools that push the boundaries of materials science.

Research Areas

First-Principles Calculations

Quantum mechanical simulations for material properties

Machine Learning

AI-driven materials discovery and property prediction

Energy Materials

Design of next-generation battery and fuel cell materials

2D Materials

Exploring novel properties of atomically thin materials
